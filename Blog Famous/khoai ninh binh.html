Khoai Lang Thang - Travel Enthusiast

Khoai Lang Thang, the nom de plume of Đinh Võ Hoài Phương, stands out as a fervent traveler and storyteller with a particular affection for the roads less traveled in Vietnam.

Khoai Lang Thang, real name Đinh Võ Hoài Phương, hails from Bến Tre and was born in 1991. The backstory of his nickname, "Wandering Potato," reflects his Mekong Delta roots and his pursuit of travel blogging, symbolizing his down-to-earth, approachable persona.

With a robust following across social media, including 2.5 million followers on Facebook, 2.29 million on YouTube, 1.7 million on TikTok, and 228 thousand on Instagram, Khoai Lang Thang has firmly established himself as a "national vlogger" through his engaging travel and culinary content.

His contributions to promoting local tourism and culture through his vlogs have made him a beloved figure online, earning him the endearing nickname "national husband" among his fans.

Who is Khoai Lang Thang?

Khoai Lang Thang, or Đinh Võ Hoài Phương, has captivated the internet with his travel vlogs. Known for his warm voice, friendly and humorous nature, and handsome, sincere appearance, he has made significant strides in his career, appearing on various inspirational TV programs and maintaining a strong social media presence.

Embarking on a Travel Blogging Journey

Although he initially pursued a career in architectural engineering, Khoai's passion for travel led him to the world of travel blogging in 2017. Starting with no prior filming or photography skills, he dedicated himself to learning and improving, driven by feedback from his audience.

Uncovering Hoa Binh with Khoai Lang Thang

In Hoa Binh, Khoai Lang Thang explores the serene landscapes and the vibrant culture of the Muong and other ethnic groups. His videos offer a glimpse into the everyday lives, traditions, and cuisines of the local communities, making his viewers feel like they're part of the journey.

Tasting Hoa Binh through Khoai's Experiences

Khoai's journeys through Hoa Binh reveal the region's culinary diversity, from traditional Muong dishes to modern Vietnamese cuisine. His storytelling brings the flavors of Hoa Binh to life, highlighting the connection between food, culture, and community.

Khoai's Must-Do Experiences in Hoa Binh

For Khoai Lang Thang, the essence of Hoa Binh lies in its rivers, mountains, and the warmth of its people. He recommends exploring the Da River by boat, visiting the Hoa Binh Dam, and immersing oneself in the local festivals to truly understand the spirit of this beautiful province.

"Hoa Binh is a testament to Vietnam's natural beauty and cultural richness, a journey into the heart of the country."

Exploring Beyond Hoa Binh with Khoai Lang Thang

While Hoa Binh is a key focus, Khoai Lang Thang's adventures span the length and breadth of Vietnam and beyond. His dedication to showcasing every destination's unique story invites his audience to discover new horizons and experiences through his eyes.
