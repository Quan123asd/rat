Đinh Võ Hoài Phương, widely known as Khoai Lang Thang, was born in 1991 in Bến Tre. His unique nickname, translating to "Wandering Potato," stems from a playful twist on his name by friends, reflecting his roots in the Mekong Delta and his love for travel and exploration.

Embracing his passion for travel, Khoai Lang Thang has become a renowned travel vlogger, capturing the hearts of millions across platforms: 2.5 million followers on Facebook, 2.29 million on YouTube, 1.7 million on TikTok, and 228 thousand on Instagram.

Who is Khoai Lang Thang?
